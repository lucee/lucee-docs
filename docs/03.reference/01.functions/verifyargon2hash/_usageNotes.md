Alias for [[function-argon2checkhash]]. 

Provided by the Crypto Extension on Lucee 7+; the underlying `Argon2CheckHash()` was originally shipped by the [Argon2 Extension](https://github.com/lucee/extension-argon2) on Lucee 5.3.8.43+ and 6.x.

For new code, prefer [[function-argon2verify]] for consistent naming with [[function-bcryptverify]] and [[function-scryptverify]].
