The encryption algorithm for which to generate the key.

The available algorithms (depending on your JVM) are 

- HmacSHA3-224
- HmacSHA384
- HmacSHA512/224
- HmacSHA512
- HmacSHA512/256
- HmacSHA3-512
- HmacSHA3-256
- HmacSHA3-384
- HmacSHA224
- ARCFOUR
- Blowfish
- HmacSHA256
- HmacSHA1
- AES
- HmacMD5
- DESede
- DES
- RC2
- ChaCha20
